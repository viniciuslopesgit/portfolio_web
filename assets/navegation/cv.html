Vinícius Lopes

born in Bahia, Brazil, 1993
lives and works in Porto, Portugal


Education / Educação

2016 Architecture and Urbanism, Centro Universitário Euro-Americano, Brasília

Exhibit Recent in: Recently, the artist participated in the 2024 Cerveira Biennial, organised by the Cerveira Biennial Art Foundation in Vila Nova de Cerveira, Portugal, curated by Helena Mendes Pereira. The artist also featured in the 20th SP-ARTE at the Biennale Pavilion - Ciccillo Matarazzo Pavilion, São Paulo, Brazil, represented by Luis Maluf Gallery. At the same gallery, they participated in the exhibition USINA in São Paulo, curated by Agnaldo Farias and Priscyla Gomes. In 2023, they showcased work at the exhibition "Primeira" at Galeria Interior, Viseu, curated by Teresa Arêde. Two solo exhibitions were presented in Porto, Portugal: "No outro dia" at Mármol Project, curated by Matias Romano, and "Descançaço" at OKNA Gallery, curated by Radu Sciliar. Additionally, they participated in the group show "Visibilidade e Cegueira" at Atelier Galeria FFAC, curated by Tales Frey.
